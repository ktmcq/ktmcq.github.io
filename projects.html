game

Utilizing an Arduino and circuit components, my partner and I used C to implement a mini version of a popular carnival game. The user can set the difficulty by turning a potentiometer and plays by striking the piezo with a mini 3D-printed mallet with the goal of hitting the sensor as hard as he or she can.
